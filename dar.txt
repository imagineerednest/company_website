3. **Web Development**: Scalable and responsive web applications (Next.js focus).
4. **App Development**: Mobile application solutions.

6. **SEO & Digital Marketing**: Search engine optimization and online brand growth.
7. **Google Business Setup**: Specialized Google Maps and business profile optimization.

## Professional Promise
- "We guide your new business every step of the way."
- High-quality design, scalable development, and powerful digital marketing strategies.
- 100% Client Satisfaction with agile development processes.

## Technical Environment
- **Framework**: Next.js 15+ (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Animations**: Framer Motion
- **Fonts**: Poppins (Google Fonts)
- **Deployment**: Production-ready with SEO and OpenGraph metadata integration.

## Project Structure
- `src/app/(pages)`: Route-grouped pages (About, Services, Portfolio, FAQ, etc.)
- `src/app/components`: Reusable UI components.
- `src/app/data`: Centralized data management for portfolio and blogs.
- `public/images`: Organized asset storage for logos, project previews, and media.
